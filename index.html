The Oxford Hackathon

OxfordHack is a hackathon that brings creative minds together to solve challenging problems. Whether you want to build an app, game, website, or simply tinker with hardware, we will provide you the space and the support of our mentors to make your idea come true!

Over a course of a weekend, you will have a chance to meet amazing people, push your limits, and unleash your potential. Whether you have a team or not, whether you already have an idea or just want to get inspired, whether this will be your first rodeo or you are a hackathon veteran, you will be able to learn and create something extraordinary.

Don’t let such an opportunity to slip through your hands and apply!

Venue

OxfordHack 2018 will be held at the Mathematical Institute at the University of Oxford.

FAQ

Where and when is Oxford Hack 2018 happening?
Oxford Hack 2018 will be held on the 24th – 25th of November at the Mathematical Institute, Woodstock Rd, Oxford OX2 6GG.
Am I eligible to attend?
Coders over the age of 18 can apply to attend the Hack.
Do I need to have a team?
No – there will be time at the start of the event of meet everyone and form teams. You can even hack solo if you want to!
How many people can be in a team?
Teams have to be made up of 1 to 4 coders.
How much does it cost?
Absolutely nothing. Oxford Hack 2018 is completely free to attend! We will provide WiFi, meals and the workspace for the event.
What do I need to bring?
All attendees must bring a valid ID for admission. Other common checklist items include: laptops, sleeping bags, chargers, and toiletries.
Where can I find accommodation and meals?
Meals are provided free of charge to all participants. There will be scheduled meal times throughout the event, held in the Mathematical Institute Cafe (located on the Mezzanine Floor of the Andrew Wiles Building). We have designated sleeping zones organised within the Mathematical Institute.
How are we judged?
Projects will be judged on creativity, practicality, technical difficulty and polish by a panel of industry judges. And don’t worry, the IP for whatever you come up with is all yours!
I have more questions!!
We’re here to help! Send us an email at team@oxfordhack.uk and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
